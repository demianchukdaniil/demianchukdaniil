My Biography

Birth

I was born on March 10, 1995 in a small town in Ukraine. It was a beautiful spring day and my parents were overjoyed to welcome their first child into the world.

Childhood

My childhood was filled with many happy memories. I loved playing with my siblings and exploring the outdoors. One of my favorite memories was when I won first place in the school science fair on May 15, 2003.

Youth

During my teenage years, I was very involved in sports and extracurricular activities. I played on the school volleyball team and participated in the drama club. One of the most significant moments of my youth was when I graduated high school on June 5, 2013.
